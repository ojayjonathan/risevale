About Risevale Kenya Tours

Discover our story, mission, and the expert team behind your Kenyan safari adventure

Our Story

Risevale Kenya Tours was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate safari enthusiasts and wildlife conservationists who shared a vision: to create transformative safari experiences in Kenya that honor the Maasai, Samburu, and other indigenous communities while preserving Kenya's extraordinary wildlife and ecosystems. What started as a small safari lodge partnership has grown into Kenya's most trusted boutique safari operator.

Over the past 14 years, we've had the privilege of guiding over 50,000 travelers through Kenya's most iconic destinationsâ€”Masai Mara, Amboseli, Tsavo, Mount Kenya, and coastal Mombasa. Our commitment to authentic experiences, wildlife conservation, and sustainable tourism has earned us numerous eco-tourism awards and the unwavering loyalty of adventurers from around the world.

Our Mission

At Risevale Kenya Tours, our mission is to craft life-changing safaris that celebrate Kenya's natural wonders and vibrant cultures. We believe that transformative travel has the power to inspire conservation, build meaningful connections between cultures, and create lasting positive impact. We are dedicated to:

  • Creating authentic, immersive Kenyan safari experiences
  • Supporting Maasai and local communities through fair compensation and cultural exchange
  • Protecting Kenya's wildlife and ecosystems through responsible tourism
  • Providing world-class service and meticulous attention to detail
  • Fostering cross-cultural understanding and respect for African traditions

Our Commitment to Kenya's Future

We believe responsible safari tourism is key to protecting Kenya's wildlife and empowering local communities. Our conservation and community initiatives include:

Community Partnership

We partner directly with Maasai villages, paying fair wages to cultural guides, supporting schools, and respecting traditional customs while creating economic opportunities.

Wildlife Conservation

We contribute 3% of tour revenue to Kenya Wildlife Service and anti-poaching initiatives, protecting elephants, rhinos, lions, and endangered species.

Ecosystem Protection

We use eco-friendly accommodations, minimize carbon footprints, and educate travelers about protecting Kenya's diverse habitats from Savanna to coast.
