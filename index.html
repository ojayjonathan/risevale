Welcome to Risevale Kenya Tours

Since 2010, Risevale has been crafting unforgettable Kenyan safari experiences. Our passion for Kenya's remarkable wildlife, pristine landscapes, and rich cultural heritage has made us a trusted partner for travelers seeking authentic African adventures.

With over a decade of experience guiding travelers through Masai Mara, Amboseli, Tsavo, and Lake Nakuru, we pride ourselves on creating thoughtfully curated safari tours that respect local Maasai and Samburu communities, preserve Kenya's extraordinary ecosystems, and provide transformative experiences for our adventurous guests.
